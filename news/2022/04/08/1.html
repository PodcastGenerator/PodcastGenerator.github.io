Podcast Generator 3.2 (and 3.1.5) has been released!

Published on 2022-04-08 by Chris Charabaruk

After nearly two years, the second update to Podcast Generator 3 has finally been released. This version introduces a number of new features and fixes various bugs. You can see the list of what's added and fixed in the new changelog.

Concurrently, version 3.1.5 has also been released for those who aren't yet ready to make the leap to 3.2. This version fixes a couple of bugs related to episode uploading and RSS feed generation.
